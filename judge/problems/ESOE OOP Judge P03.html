Green Crud

Deadline

2019-06-24 23:59:59.

Description

Green crud is a new kind of bacterium that has just been discovered and named by scientists. To better understand them, scientists have carried out a seires of experiments over the growth of the green crud population.

It is observed that the green crud population grows every five days, and that it grows in the same manner as the Fibonacci sequence does.

For your information, the Fibonacci sequence is defined as follows:

{F1=F2=1Fi+2=Fi+Fi+1, for i≥1 { F 1 = F 2 = 1 F i + 2 = F i + F i + 1 ,  for  i ≥ 1

Hence, for example, if the green crud population starts out as 10 10 pounds of cruds, then in 5 5 days, there will still be 10 10 pounds of cruds; in 10 10 days, there will be 20 20 pounds of cruds; in 15 15 days, 30 30 pounds; in 20 20 days, 50 50 pounds; and so forth.

In this problem, you are asked to implement the GreenCrud GreenCrud class.

In GreenCrud GreenCrud , there is only a single method that you should implement, which is calPopulation calPopulation , and whose function is to calculate the size of the green crud population after a given amount of days, starting at a given initial size.

Input Format

GreenCrud gc = new GreenCrud(); GreenCrud gc = new GreenCrud();

gc.calPopulation(initialSize, days) gc.calPopulation(initialSize, days) , where intialSize intialSize and days days are integers, and that 1≤initialSize≤10000 1 ≤ initialSize ≤ 10000 and 1≤days≤100 1 ≤ days ≤ 100 .

Output Format

gc.calPopulation(initialSize, days) gc.calPopulation(initialSize, days) should return an integer, which is the size of the green crud population after days days days, starting at an initial size of initialSize initialSize .

Sample Input

GreenCrud gc = new GreenCrud();

System.out.println(gc.calPopulation(6, 15));
System.out.println(gc.calPopulation(10, 17));
System.out.println(gc.calPopulation(3, 20));
