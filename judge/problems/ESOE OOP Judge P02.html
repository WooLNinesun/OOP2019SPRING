Leap Year

Deadline

Description

A leap year, also known as an intercalary year or a bissextile year, is a year containing one additional day added to keep the calendar year synchronized with the astronomical or seasonal year.

In the Gregorian calendar, the standard calendar in most of the world, the schemes of leap years are as follows:

Every year that is exactly divisible by 400 400 is a leap year.
Every year that is exactly divisible by 4 4 but not by 100 100 is a leap year.

In this problem, you are asked to implement the IsLeapYear IsLeapYear class.

In IsLeapYear IsLeapYear , there is only a single method that you should implement, which is determine determine , and whose function is to determine whether or not a given year is a leap year.
