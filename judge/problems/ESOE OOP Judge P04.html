Sentence Processor

Deadline

2019-06-24 23:59:59.

Description

We often need to remove some duplicated words from a sentence or to replace some occurrences of a word in a sentence with another word. The world will be a lot better if we can write a program to help us do such tedious work.

In this problem, you are asked to implement the SentenceProcessor SentenceProcessor class.

In SentenceProcessor SentenceProcessor , there are two methods that you should implement, which are removeDuplicatedWords removeDuplicatedWords and replaceWord replaceWord , respectively.

In details, removeDuplicatedWords removeDuplicatedWords should remove all duplicated words (leaving only the one that occurs first) in a given sentence, and replaceWord replaceWord should replace all occurrences of a given word in a given sentence with another given word.

These methods are case-sensitive, which means that words in upper- and lowercase should be treated as different words.

You may assume that all given sentences consist of only English alphabets (may be in lower case, upper case, or capitalized) and white spaces.

Note that the resulting sentences should not contain any redundant white spaces; that is, for example, if removeDuplicatedWords removeDuplicatedWords were to work on "abc abc" "abc abc" , then the resulting sentence should be "abc" "abc" but not "abc " "abc " (note the trailing white space).

Input Format

SentenceProcessor sp = new SentenceProcessor(); SentenceProcessor sp = new SentenceProcessor();

sp.removeDuplicatedWords(sentence) sp.removeDuplicatedWords(sentence) , where sentence sentence is a string.
sp.replaceWord(target, replacement, sentence) sp.replaceWord(target, replacement, sentence) , where target target , replacement replacement and sentence sentence are strings.

Output Format

sp.removeDuplicatedWords(sentence) sp.removeDuplicatedWords(sentence) should return a string, which is sentence sentence after removal of all duplicated words (leaving only the one that occurs first).
sp.replaceWord(target, replacement, sentence) sp.replaceWord(target, replacement, sentence) should return a string, which is sentence sentence after replacement of all occurrences of target target with replacement replacement .

Sample Input

SentenceProcessor sp = new SentenceProcessor();

System.out.println(sp.removeDuplicatedWords("Hello Hello World I love love the World I lovelove the World"));
System.out.println(sp.removeDuplicatedWords("Buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo buffalo buffalo Buffalo buffalo"));
System.out.println(sp.removeDuplicatedWords("a a la a la carte A la La carte Carte A a la la"));
System.out.println(sp.replaceWord("major", "minor", "The major problem is how to sing in A major"));
System.out.println(sp.replaceWord("on", "off", "Turn on the television I want to keep the television on"));
System.out.println(sp.replaceWord("love", "hate", "I love the World I lovelove the Love"));

Buffalo buffalo
a la carte A La Carte
The minor problem is how to sing in A minor
Turn off the television I want to keep the television off
